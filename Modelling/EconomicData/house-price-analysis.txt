European AVM alliance
Discussing incumbent use of index linked house price valuations. Bias related to the initial valuation being used within the 
indexed method. Has the inherent flaw of confirmation bias. The method outlines that the future for valuations within Europe 
will be the use of AVMs. These models should represent more accurate valuations of real estate. Also, the AVM structure helps 
to provide a confidence level to the valuations, which can help users to understand the level of volatility around a valuation.
